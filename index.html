Nigeria

Nigeria is a country located in West Africa, bordered by Benin, Chad, Cameroon, and Niger, with a coastline along the Atlantic Ocean. It is the most populous country in Africa and has a diverse culture with over 500 ethnic groups. Nigeria's official language is English, and its economy is one of the largest in Africa, driven by oil, agriculture, and telecommunications. The capital city is Abuja, while Lagos is the largest city and a major economic hub.
